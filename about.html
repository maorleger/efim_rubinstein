Meet Dr. Rubinstein

Dr. Rubinstein with his manager Tamilla

Dr. Rubinstein is a recognized expert in aesthetic, general and implants dentistry. He has been trained with world-renowned dental instructors around the world. Along with a private practice, He shares his expertise and experience as a Clinical Assistant Professor at New York University College of Dentistry.

He recieved his D.D.S. degree from New York University College of Dentistry, Advanced Program. His training also includes Tel-Aviv University 1991-92 and in Kazakhstan (former USSR) as a Physician-Stomatologist in 1983-1988.

Dr. Rubinstein is committed to continuing education and always utilizes the latest techniques to make your treatment comfortable and easy.

Dr. Rubinstein is a member of:

  • American Dental Association
  • American Dental Education Association
  • American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Academy of General Dentistry
